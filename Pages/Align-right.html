What is Align Right?

Align Right is a productivity tool that aligns the right edges of selected elements or tags in Autodesk Revit with a single click.

The tool automatically positions all selected objects so that their right edges are aligned, creating clean and consistent layouts throughout your project.

Designed for everyday BIM workflows, Align Right reduces manual alignment work, improves documentation quality, and helps create professional-looking drawings.

How to Use Align Right?

  1. Open Align Settings and choose the alignment mode you want to use.
  2. Select the elements or tags you want to align.
  3. Click the Align Right button in the BIM Plugin ribbon.
  4. The right edges of the selected elements will be aligned automatically.

Note: Align Right works with supported Revit elements and tags. The alignment result depends on the mode selected in Align Settings.
