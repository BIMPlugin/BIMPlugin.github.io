What is Who Did That?

Who Did That? is a productivity tool that displays Worksharing information for any element in an Autodesk Revit model.

With a single click, the tool shows who created the selected element, who last modified it, and whether it is currently owned by another user, making collaboration and troubleshooting much easier.

Designed for everyday BIM workflows, Who Did That? improves team coordination, simplifies model management, and helps quickly identify ownership and editing history of Revit elements.

How to Use Who Did That?

  1. Click the Who Did That? button in the BIM Plugin ribbon.
  2. Select any element in the Revit model.
  3. BIM Plugin will display information about:
    • Who created the element.
    • Who last modified the element.
    • Who currently owns (has checked out) the element.

Note: This tool is intended for workshared Revit projects. The available information depends on the project's worksharing data.
